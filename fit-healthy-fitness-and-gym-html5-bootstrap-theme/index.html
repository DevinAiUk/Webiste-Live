We Offer the Best of Services

We are committed to providing compassionate and professional care for individuals with disabilities. Explore our services and discover how we can help you or your loved ones live a more fulfilling life.

  • About Us: Learn about our mission to support and empower people with disabilities. Meet our dedicated team and find out about our history and the values that guide our work.
  • Our Services: Offering comprehensive services to support individuals with developmental disabilities in achieving their life goals through personal empowerment and community involvement.
  • Homemaker Companion: Our services assist individuals with daily household tasks and activities, fostering independence and improving quality of life.
  • Transportation: Safe and reliable services to support access to community resources, medical appointments, and social activities.
  • Assisted Living Facility: Comfortable and supportive living arrangements in our state-of-the-art facility, providing 24/7 care and a variety of life-enhancing activities.
  • Adult Day Care: Engaging day programs that offer social, recreational, and health services in a friendly and nurturing environment.

At APD, we strive for excellence in every service we provide, driven by our commitment to enhance and empower the lives of our clients.

Personalized Care Programs

Our personalized care programs are designed to meet the unique needs of each client. We focus on providing individualized support that empowers our clients, fostering independence and enhancing quality of life through tailored solutions.

Community Engagement Initiatives

Our community engagement initiatives aim to create a more inclusive and supportive environment. We engage in various activities and partnerships that promote the well-being and active participation of our clients within the community.

Advanced Technological Solutions

We harness advanced technological solutions to improve the services we provide. By integrating innovative technologies into our programs, we enhance our operational efficiency and the effectiveness of our care, ensuring that every client benefits from the latest advancements.
